About the series

Dark is a German science fiction thriller television series that premiered on Netflix on December 1, 2017. Created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese, it is notable for being Netflix's first German-language original series. The show concluded with its third season on June 27, 2020, totaling 26 episodes across three seasons.

Plot overview

Set in the fictional German town of Winden, Dark explores the complex relationships among four interconnected families as they grapple with the mysterious disappearance of children. The narrative delves into themes of time travel, existentialism, and the impact of human decisions across generations. The story intricately weaves together various timelines, revealing how past actions influence the present and future.
